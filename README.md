# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Description

This project analyzes Blinkit grocery sales data using Power BI to understand sales performance, customer preferences, outlet distribution, and product category trends. An interactive dashboard was created to visualize key business metrics and generate actionable insights.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze total sales, average sales, and item performance
* Identify top-performing product categories and outlet types
* Understand customer ratings and outlet establishment trends
* Visualize sales distribution across locations and outlet sizes
* Build an interactive dashboard for business insights

## 🛠 Skills & Tools

* Power BI
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Modeling
* DAX Measures
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence Reporting

## 📦 Deliverables

* Interactive Power BI Dashboard
* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Average Sales, Ratings, Items)
* Outlet & Product Category Analysis
* Sales Trend Visualization
* Customer Rating Analysis
* Dynamic Filters and Slicers

## 📈 Key Insights

* Total sales performance and item distribution were analyzed across multiple outlet types
* Certain product categories generated significantly higher revenue
* Medium-sized outlets contributed a major share of overall sales
* Customer ratings helped identify better-performing outlet segments

## 🚀 Impact

Provided clear business insights into sales trends, outlet performance, and customer behavior, helping improve strategic decision-making and operational analysis.
